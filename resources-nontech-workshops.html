  • • Building confidence through goal setting and reflection
  • • Career exploration and resume development
  • • Leveraging campus resources and identity-affirming communities

Tips for Implementation

Institutions are encouraged to:

  • • Invite student leaders, identity-based student orgs, or career center staff to facilitate sessions
  • • Incorporate presentations from academic support centers, wellness staff, or DEI offices
  • • Use reflective journaling or discussion prompts to reinforce personal development
  • • Frame each session around a practical outcome (e.g., finding a mentor, joining a group)
  • • Mix workshops with social events to foster connection and engagement
