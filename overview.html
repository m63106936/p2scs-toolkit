P2SCS: A Model for Equity-Driven Computer Science Retention

The Pathways to Success in Computer Science (P2SCS) program is a multi-component academic support initiative designed to improve persistence, academic performance, and identity development among underrepresented CS students. This toolkit provides the blueprint for institutions to replicate or adapt P2SCS to fit your local context.

Explore the Model

Computer Science Immersion

A summer bridge experience that fosters social belonging, academic momentum, and early exposure to CS principles through collaborative workshops, identity-affirming discussions, and mentorship.

Weekly Check-ins

Structured weekly meetings between students and mentors or coordinators provide a safe space to monitor academic progress, discuss well-being, and develop self-regulated learning strategies.

Workshops

Both technical and non-technical workshops empower students with academic tools, project-based learning, career preparation, and peer collaboration, anchored in educational psychology principles.

Targeted Academic Support

Peer learning, supplemental instruction, and guided study groups reinforce conceptual understanding and reduce cognitive overload in gateway CS courses.

Professional Exposure

Industry visits with diverse and alumni panels give students tangible insight into real-world CS careers and pathways, reinforcing their sense of future self in the field.
